# Ad Format Ideation

Create real format diversity, not one script skeleton wearing different clothes.

## Process

1. Check campaign memory, `inventory/`, scripts, and outputs so new concepts are actually new.
2. Identify what must stay constant: audience, offer, qualifying facts, brand, compliance, and CTA.
3. Vary at least three axes:
   - messenger or point of view
   - visual technique
   - hook mechanism
   - proof type
   - CTA framing
4. Present each concept with:
   - a 2-3 sentence opener
   - messenger
   - compact beat arc
   - visual technique
   - proof media
   - production route
   - why it differs from shipped work
5. Recommend a first batch whose concepts are maximally different from one another.
6. After approval, route production through `video-production`.

## Messenger Palette

- customer/user story
- friend or family member
- expert or advocate
- skeptical interviewer
- ex-staff/insider
- local commentator
- reporter or field correspondent
- case-worker/guide
- chorus of short voices
- faceless narrator

Do not impersonate a real professional or customer without permission. For regulated campaigns, load the domain/compliance skill before choosing a messenger.

## Visual Technique Palette

- podcast/interview
- direct notice
- news or field report
- checklist or quiz
- myth versus fact
- comment/debate interface
- phone thread or voicemail
- product demonstration
- form or workflow demonstration
- found document or archival reveal
- then/now split screen
- PIP commentator over proof
- stacked proof plus presenter
- kinetic typography/data reveal
- clay/stop-motion metaphor
- 2D motion-graphic explainer
- montage with voiceover
- symbolic/object-led story
- screen-recorded comparison

## Hook Palette

- specific question
- surprising constraint
- confession
- objection reversal
- warning/notice
- visual reveal
- overheard line
- demonstration result
- one-word filter
- numbered promise
- myth correction
- comment response

## Quality Rules

- A title is not enough; write the opener so pacing and voice can be judged.
- Preserve a winner's framing and beat logic only as scaffolding.
- Change the discovery mechanism and speaker logic, not just nouns and synonyms.
- Make proof appropriate to the claim; do not substitute generic filler.
- Keep concepts producible in the current tool stack.
- Campaign compliance stays in its domain skill or memory, not in this general ideation skill.
