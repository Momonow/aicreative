# Video Quality Bar

## Story

- The opening establishes relevance quickly.
- Each scene advances the same argument.
- Proof appears where the spoken claim needs support.
- The CTA is clear and proportional to the content.

## Visuals

- People and scenes remain continuous.
- B-roll is readable at feed size and emotionally appropriate.
- Cuts feel intentional; no accidental flashes or empty filler.
- Captions and overlays respect faces, products, interfaces, and safe areas.

## Audio

- Dialogue is intelligible and consistent.
- Voice identity does not change unexpectedly.
- No clipped consonants, inserted words, music contamination, pumping, or true-peak overs.
- Silence and room tone feel natural.

## Mobile

- Review at actual phone scale.
- Essential text is readable without pausing.
- No caption line runs into the viewport edge.
- Platform UI will not cover the CTA or key evidence.

## Delivery

- Correct duration, dimensions, frame rate, and audio stream.
- Clean master preserved.
- Caption/disclaimer variants named clearly.
- Every-frame and transition QA passed.
