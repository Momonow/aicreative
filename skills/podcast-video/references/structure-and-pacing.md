# Structure And Pacing

A strong 30-60 second podcast video usually moves through:

1. conversational cold open
2. specific relevance or diagnosis/problem
3. explanation or discovery
4. proof media
5. one or two objections
6. clear next action

Do not force every script into this exact order. Preserve the source's persuasive function while changing the discovery mechanism and sentence structure.

Chunk generated dialogue near complete sentence or clause boundaries. Keep word density consistent; long chunks rush and short underfilled chunks invite improvisation.

In the edit, let proof inserts breathe. Back-to-back inserts may share an exact frame boundary; otherwise leave a deliberate host run, not a single-frame return.
