## Compliance hot-list — NEVER replicate these
- Equivalence/branded comparison: "same ingredient as the big brands" (1445); "a fraction of the cost of branded options" (1421/26/27/28/29); "5x cheaper / 2x faster" + "$1,349/month" anchoring (1434/1473); "competidores" (1430).
- Banned claims: "Clinically proven / 94.6% success rate / 21+ lbs in 90 days / RESULTS GUARANTEED" (1466); "miracle pills" (1489).
- Dramatic/specific result: "I lost 60lbs in 7 weeks" (1451); "30-50 Lbs before summer" (1483/1488); "up to 20% body weight" (1430).
- Guarantee: "Money-back Guarantee" (1448/1459/1470); "Lose weight or your money back" (1459); "30% OFF FOR LIFE/FOREVER" (1420/1449); "Price Lock Guarantee" (1476/1485/1490/1491 — soft, but drop the word).
- Branded name: "Mounjaro" in 1482 copy (verify/remove).
- Format/visual risks: fake video scrubber (1423/1424); Google-logo "5.0 rating" (1484); suited man as implied clinician (1459); fitness-model before/after-adjacent bodies (1420/1449).
- Most price-table cards are MISSING the on-image compounded-med footnote — we always add it.

NOTE: "semaglutide" / "tirzepatide" are ALLOWED compound names (not brands) — used throughout the swipes and on the offer LP.
